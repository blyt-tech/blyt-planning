## Outcome

The master_hook DISPATCHER composes correctly under all five variants.
Carts run to completion. No crashes. No spurious budget trips.

Real overhead measurement requires rv32emu's syscall 113 (clock_gettime)
support — currently a ~2-3 line patch to rv32emu's syscall.c. This is the
single follow-up gating Stage 1 step 4's quantitative comparison against
Spike G's 3.74 ms p99 ceiling on `doom_tick`. The dispatch composition
itself is validated.
